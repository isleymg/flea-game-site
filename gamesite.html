FLEA INVASION

Protect your pup from an infestation.

STORY

Flea Invasion is a mobile strategy game designed not for the faint-hearted. Nasty, disgusting fleas harass your pet. It is up to you ultimately to destroy all the fleas before your four-legged companion dies from the infestation.

A health bar indicates the level of infestation and overall health of your pooch. Pick your attacks and defenses from a selection of cards. Depending on the type of card you choose, the effects of your attack or defense will vary.
